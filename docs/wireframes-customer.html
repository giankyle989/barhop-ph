The Pool Bar

📍 2F Electra House, Esteban St., Legaspi Village, Makati City, Metro Manila
🌿 Outdoor Seating 🍹 Cocktail Menu 🎵 Live Music 🅿️ Parking Available 📶 Wi-Fi Available 💳 Cards Accepted

About The Pool Bar

The Pool Bar is Makati's beloved open-air cocktail lounge perched above the buzzing streets of Legaspi Village. With its signature pool table, curated craft cocktail menu, and laid-back atmosphere, it has become a staple of the Makati bar scene since opening its doors in 2014.

Whether you're unwinding after a long workday with one of their classic margaritas or catching live acoustic sets on weekend evenings, The Pool Bar offers a refreshing escape from the city's hustle. The kitchen serves up an impressive selection of pulutan and bar bites designed to pair perfectly with your drinks.
